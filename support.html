The MIT Environmental Solutions Initiative Rapid Response Group receives support from several public and private sources. Funders do not participate in editorial activities or any other activities and do not interfere with the positions taken by the group. The ESI is governed by the rules and regulations of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. The RRG values diversity and promotes equity and inclusion as core principles as guided by MIT and the ESI Principles of Conduct and Engagement.

The ESI RRG is a nonpartisan entity of the MIT Environmental Solutions Initiative. Views held and expressed by the ESI RRG are the product of the RRG alone and do not necessarily reflect any policy stance or commitment of the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. The ESI RRG reports to the Director of the ESI and abides by MIT Federal Lobbying Regulations as dictated the Lobbying Disclosure Act of 1995. For more information see Office of the Vice President for Research: research.mit.edu.
